Our Story

From Kaffa forests to a modern Ethiopian coffee house

It began in the forests of Kaffa, where stories of Kaldi and dancing goats still frame the mystery of coffee's origin. Kaloss was built to bring that origin story forward with honesty, beauty, and economic fairness.

We roast and present Ethiopian coffee as a living culture, not just a commodity. Every lot is chosen to reflect the place, people, and ritual behind the cup.

2010

Founded in Addis Ababa

Kaloss began with a simple belief: the birthplace of coffee should be represented with dignity, beauty, and fair value.

2015

First export lots

Our first micro-lots from Yirgacheffe and Sidama reached international roasters with full farmer attribution.

2020

Direct trade commitment

We formalized long-term sourcing relationships and premium payments tied to quality and sustainability goals.

2024

Global cultural storytelling

Kaloss expanded into a fuller Ethiopian coffee experience that honors ceremony, craft, and origin.
